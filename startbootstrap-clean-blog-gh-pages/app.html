Invincible Gauntlet

Fight through the gauntlet and survive until the final showdown with Omni-Man.

Round 1: vs Mauler Twins

How to Play

  • Attack deals damage and charges your special meter.
  • Guard cuts the next incoming attack.
  • Recover heals you, but can leave you open.
  • Special Move unlocks at 3 charge.
  • You can now see the enemy’s next move, so play strategically.
  • Beat the gauntlet: Mauler Twins → Sequid Horde → Battle Beast → Omni-Man.
